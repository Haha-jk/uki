
program
    declaration*

declaration
    | Identifier parameters? Colon expression
    | statement

parameters
    Identifier (Comma Identifier)*

arguments
    expression (Comma expression)*

statement
    | expression
    | when-statement
    | loop-statement
    | procedure

procedure
    (Indent | LeftCurlyBracket) declaration* (Dedent | RightCurlyBracket)

when-statement
    WhenKeyword expression procedure (ThenKeyword expression procedure)* (ThenKeyword procedure)?

loop-statement
    LoopKeyword procedure

expression
    assignment

assignment
    (primary Dot)? Identifier Equal logical-or

or
    and (Question and)*

and
    equality (Ampersand equality)*

equality
    comparison ((ExclamationEqual | EqualEqual) comparison)*

comparison
    term ((LessThan | ExclamationLessThan | MoreThan | ExclamationMoreThan) term )* 

term
    factor ((Minus | Plus) factor)*

factor
    unary ((Slash | Asterisk) unary)*

unary
    (Exclamation | Minus | Plus) (unary | call)

call
    

primary
    | RightKeyword
    | WrongKeyword
    | Number
    | String
    | Identifier
    | LeftRoundBracket expression RightRoundBracket
